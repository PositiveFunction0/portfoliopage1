LOVELY FAYTE ANDAYA

Lovely Fayte R. Andaya was born on September 17, 2003, and is currently 20 years old. She resides at Citta Grande Subdivision, Phase 2, Lot 1 Block 4, Via Romagna Street, Lucena City. Lovely is a 3rd-year college student, taking up Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, at Manuel S. Enverga University Foundation. In her free time, Lovely enjoys watching anime, playing online games, and listening to music. She also likes spending time with her family and friends and socializing with other people, as she considers herself an extrovert. Moreover, she loves playing badminton as her favorite sport. Lovely lives by two mottos: "There are no shortcuts to any place worth going" and "My best therapy is music." These mottos inspire her to work hard and pursue her goals, as well as to find comfort and solace in the power of music.
